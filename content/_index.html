GYC West is excited to present its eleventh-annual conference with the theme: “Reflect:The daily experience”. This year’s conference will focus on the practical side of following Christ in our everyday lives, and letting HIS light shine through us. On June 22nd through the 24th, young people from all over the region will gather at Pine Hills Academy in Auburn, CA for an inspiring mix of powerful messages, earnest prayer, outreach, and Christian fellowship.

Let your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works, and glorify your Father which is in heaven.

Matthew 5:16

This year's GYC West is being held at Pine Hills Adventist Academy.

13500 Richards Lane, Auburn, CA 95603

Be sure to bring your Bible and notepaper as this year's content is designed to bring you not only closer to our Savior, but also to give you the tools you need to become a strong worker for Him at your home and at your church.
