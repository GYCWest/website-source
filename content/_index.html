Each year GYC West hosts a young adult retreat to gain strength in the faith, network safely with like-minded believers, and seek a closer communion with our Father. For 2024 the theme will be "Turning Hearts." More details about this event and the theme to come.

Join us in 2024 to come away from your work and routines and seek revival together.

This year's GYC West is being held at Sierra Pines Camp, up in the beautiful Sierra Nevada mountains just south of Lake Tahoe.

7169 Sierra Pines Rd, Twin Bridges, CA 95721

A note about GPS: Google Maps has been proven to be accurate, however it tries to direct you off of Sierra Pines Road too early and onto a blocked road. Follow the road farther down. We will have GWC West signs up to help guide you.

Expect daytime temperatures in the 65° to 85° range and lows in the 40°s, with dry, mountain air and lots of sun.
