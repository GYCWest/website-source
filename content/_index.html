GYC West 2018 is in the works. Based on asking you about how you would like to see GYC West move forward along with a lot of prayer and inspiration from God, we are reworking how the yearly session is being held: we plan to create a much more social, team environment with a lot less lecture and a lot more doing. Needless to say, we are very excited about what's coming up - so stay tuned!

This year's GYC West is being held at Pine Hills Adventist Academy.

13500 Richards Lane, Auburn, CA 95603

Be sure to bring your Bible and notepaper as this year's content is designed to bring you not only closer to our Savior, but also to give you the tools you need to become a strong worker for Him at your home and at your church.
