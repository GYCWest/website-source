Jeffrey Maxwell

Getting comfortable with natural remedies
& CPR fundamentals

Jeffrey Maxwell's experience with natural remedies and medical missionary work goes back to 2007 when he started an apprenticeship with a lifestyle center located in the foothills of the Appalachians in north Georgia where he became head male therapist. Since then, he has been doing gospel medical missionary work in some capacity for over 13 years. He currently serves as Risk Management Director and Associate Treasurer for the Northern California Conference.

Getting comfortable with natural remedies - Get a general understanding of what God's simple natural remedies are. Also, you will see, first hand, easy remedies you can do. Jeffrey will also be sharing other tips, tricks, and hacks when it comes to doing remedies picked up through fifteen years of experience. You won't want to miss this one!

CPR fundamentals - If you'd like to learn how to do CPR in an emergency, this is the class for you. Jeffrey is a certified instructor of CPR/1st Aid/AED training. This class will go over the basics when it comes to giving cardio-pulmonary resuscitation (CPR), first aid with choking, and using an automated external defibrillator (AED). Each attendee will be able to practice these skills with hands-on assessments. NOTE: attendees will NOT be officially certified or re-certified in CPR/1st Aid/AED.
