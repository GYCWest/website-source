Each year GYC West hosts a young adult retreat to gain strength in the faith, network safely with like-minded believers, and seek a closer communion with our Father. For 2024 the theme will be "Turning Hearts." More details about this event and the theme to come.

Join us in 2024 to come away from your work and routines and seek revival together.

Jeff Walper

Jeff Walper has been serving the Lord since 1998. He was given a piece of gospel literature in a bar in North Atlanta, which he read while drinking some beer and watching TV. The Holy Spirit touched his heart that early morning with the impression that He was calling him to surrender his life to Jesus and serve Him as a missionary. Jeff has been serving Jesus for the past 25 years and is currently the Outreach Pastor at Granite Bay Hilltop SDA Church. He has been married to his lovely wife Jodi Walper for 18 years and has three precious girls—Olivia 12, Rubi 10, and Elin 9. Jeff enjoys family time, gardening, camping, and hiking.

RJ Gamboa

RJ Gamboa is passionate about Jesus and has devoted his life to sharing the gospel. He is a representative for the General Conference Initiative “Sidewalk Evangelism”, serves as an evangelism director for Outpost Centers International, and is the co-founder of Go Beyond Podcast. He lives in Northern California with his beautiful wife, Charinette.

Gabriel Arruda

Gabriel Arruda, the president of Freedom Health Ministries, has been involved in outreach and preaching the everlasting gospel for 20 years. Gabriel specializes in training churches on how to facilitate revival, do effective evangelism, and engage in medical missionary work in their communities.

Calvin Gardiner

At the age of 20, Calvin dedicated his life to following God. Between the lavish life of the Hollywood dream and life with God, he realized life with God was of more value than having money, fame, and status. After years of working with youth and young adults, his passion led him to start the “Go Beyond Podcast” that helps make the Bible relevant in today's society.

This year's GYC West is being held at Sierra Pines Camp, up in the beautiful Sierra Nevada mountains just south of Lake Tahoe.

7169 Sierra Pines Rd, Twin Bridges, CA 95721

A note about GPS: Google Maps has been proven to be accurate, however it tries to direct you off of Sierra Pines Road too early and onto a blocked road. Follow the road farther down. We will have GWC West signs up to help guide you.

Expect daytime temperatures in the 65° to 85° range and lows in the 40°s, with dry, mountain air and lots of sun.
