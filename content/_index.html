Dee Casper

Dee Casper is Director of the CORE Evangelism Training Program. Dee lived 30 minutes from 3ABN World Headquarters for 21 years and had never heard of a Seventh-Day Adventist, but by the grace of God, he found the message through 3ABN TV beginning in the fall of 2006. He was baptized in 2010 at the ARISE Cornerstone program, and has been involved in ministry ever since. He has served as a Bible worker, been a Bible teacher in an academy setting, and has taught in different schools of evangelism. He loves investing in young people, and opening their eyes to the beauty of the Everlasting Gospel, and the value that God places on each of them individually.

Dr. Diane Burnett

Doctor Diane Burnett has been in the health field for over 35 years. Dr Burnett graduated from the University of Arizona College of Medicine, where she also trained in Family Practice residency. Afterwards she served as a missionary doctor for Native Americans in a variety of capacities. She served as Medical Director at Uchee Pines Lifestyle Institute in the past with Dr. Agatha Thrash as a colleague. Currently she does outpatient care specializing in Lifestyle Medicine, educational health and spiritual programs, locally in Arizona and traveling throughout the United States, Canada, and other foreign countries.

This year's GYC West is being held at Albion Field Station, mere walking distance from the Pacific ocean.

34000 Albion St, Albion, CA 95410

Expect temperatures in the 50° to 70° range, with refreshingly moist ocean winds and lots of sun.

Be sure to bring an active mind, your Bible, and notepaper as this year's content is designed to bring you not only closer to our Savior, but also to give you the tools you need to become a strong worker for Him at your home and at your church.

Please be aware that we will be recording and live streaming our events, so please dress modestly.

There are limited accommodations, so please register early!

Pricing summary

Registration is $60 per person per night, and there are a variety of housing and meal options:

A shared cabin is $20* without a kitchenette or $40* with a kitchenette. Tent camping is free, however there are no facilities for RV parking, and limited on-site parking. One tent site is limited to no more than 4 people per tent.

Prepared meals are available varying between $9 and $10 per meal*. Preregistration for the meals is required, on-site meal registrations will be limited. The first scheduled meal is Thursday breakfast, the last is a Sunday Brunch - each of which are the only scheduled meals for those days. The intervening days each have breakfast, lunch, and supper options. You are welcome to bring your own meals if you wish.

Please note that due to limited space housing locations will be assigned.

*Prices are per person per night.
