2022 - CPR: Christ's Plan of Rescue

GYC West Yearly Main Event

Each year GYC West hosts a young adult retreat to gain strength in the faith, network safely with like-minded believers, and seek a closer communion with our Father. For 2022 the theme is "CPR: Christ's Plan of Rescue."

Speakers:

  • Janet Brandauer
  • Paul Coneff
  • Todd and Patti Guthrie
  • Dwayne Lemon
  • Jeffrey Maxwell
  • Gabriel & Vonnelle McClover
  • Hannah Rasmussen

Location: Mt. Hope Bible Camp, CA.

Text: "That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations." — Psalm 67:2

"It was His mission to bring to men complete restoration; He came to give them health and peace and perfection of character." — Ministry of Healing, p17

2021 - No Limit… to what God can do

GYC West Yearly Main Event

Each year GYC West hosts a young adult retreat to gain strength in the faith, network safely with like-minded believers, and seek a closer communion with our Father. For 2021 the theme is "No Limit… to what God can do." During this past year many people have found themselves confronted with questions and concerns regarding God and His power. Has COVID limited God's ability to work in our lives? Does the division and fear in our country and the world limit God's ability to work through us? We believe nothing can limit our Lord's work when we follow His Word and we want to encourage you to trust Him alone.

Location: Sierra Pines Camp, CA.

Text: "There is no limit to the usefulness of the one who, putting self aside, makes room for the working of the Holy Spirit upon his heart, and lives a life wholly consecrated to God." — Testimonies for the Church, volume 8, p19

2020 — 20/20 Vision, Purpose refocused

GYC West Yearly Main Event

God has for each of us a clear vision, an elevated purpose. He wants those who will "press towards the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus." Today, as young adults, our vision can be set! Our purpose can be firm! But is our purpose firm in Christ? Is our vision clear? And what is our purpose? How can we live to glorify God in the 21st century?

Do you want 20/20 vision? Come join us this year at Barlow Group Camp where we will have our purpose refocused and our vision set on higher things.

Location: Barlow Group Camp, CA.

Text: "Forgetting those things which are behind, I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus." — Philippians 3:13, 14

2019 — Let These Stand

GYC West Yearly Main Event

God has called every generation to stand unflinchingly for Christ and His truth, and today is no different. Are you ready to stand though the heavens fall? Come aside with GYC West this summer for an unforgettable, outdoor experience down by the riverside at the Albion Field Station to prepare to stand in these last days for the final conflict ahead.

Location: Albion, CA.

Text: "Wherefore take unto you the whole armour of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand." — Ephesians 6:13 (KJV)
